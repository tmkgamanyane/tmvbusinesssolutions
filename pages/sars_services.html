SARS Services Requirements

Everything you need to know about tax registration and compliance in South Africa

Tax Registration

Requirements:

  • Completed IT77 Form or online registration
  • Certified copy of ID/Passport of owner/directors
  • Business address and contact details
  • Company documents (e.g., CIPC Registration Certificate for companies)
  • Bank account details (in the business name)
  • Details of business activities

VAT Registration

Requirements:

  • Valid Tax Reference Number (from tax registration)
  • Certified ID/Passport of applicant
  • Proof of banking details (recent statement)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement)
  • Financial projections OR 3-6 months of bank statements (to prove turnover will exceed R1 million)

Tax and VAT Returns

Requirements:

  • Monthly/periodic financial records (sales and purchases)
  • All valid tax invoices from suppliers
  • Bank statements for the period
  • Payroll records (if applicable)
  • Previous return documents (for consistency)

Maintaining Tax/VAT Compliance

Requirements:

  • Organized and complete financial records
  • Valid tax invoices for all transactions
  • Regular reconciliation of books
  • Up-to-date understanding of tax laws
  • Accurate and timely submission of returns

UIF/PAYE/SDL Registration (as an Employer)

Requirements:

  • Business Tax Number
  • Certified ID of the employer/representative
  • Details of all employees (ID numbers, gross salaries)
  • Proof of business banking details
  • Completed EMP101 form

Import and Export License (SARS Customs)

Requirements:

  • Business Tax Reference Number
  • Certified copy of company director's ID
  • CIPC Registration Documents
  • Proof of business address
  • Bank confirmation letter
  • Specific details about goods to be imported/exported

Appointing a SARS Representative (ToR01/02 Form)

Requirements:

  • Completed ToR01 (Appointment) and ToR02 (Acceptance) forms
  • Tax Reference Number of the principal (business owner)
  • Tax Reference Number of the representative (us/accountant)
  • Certified ID copies for both parties

Customs Registration (Client Registration)

Requirements:

  • Business Tax Reference Number
  • CIPC Registration Documents (for companies)
  • Certified ID of owner/directors
  • Proof of physical business address
  • Contact details and information about the nature of intended customs activities

For more information, visit the SARS official website
